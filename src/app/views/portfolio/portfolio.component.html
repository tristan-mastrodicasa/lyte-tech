Game Watch

Game watch is an event organising app for multiplayer games. It is mean't to fill the demand for esports teams who want to organise competitions and record their results. It is easily linkable with discord.

The events on Game Watch are linkable through URL's and can be accessed from desktop browsers where most "gamers" reside. Mobile users have the option to download the app and recieve notifications when an event is about to happen.

Plink

Plink is a social media app that allows users to create funny captions on images of each other. These captioned images are listed below the image and ranked based on how many stars they have. Users earn points for every star they accumulate.

Each captioned image is linkable and downloadable. This allows users who do not have the app to engage with other users on the site. Since Plink is a social media app, the ability to gain users without them needing to download the app is invaluable.
